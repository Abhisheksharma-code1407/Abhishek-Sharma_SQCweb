AI Powered Robot Dog – Chi-Chi

Chi-Chi is an AI-powered robotic dog developed by the technical club Super Quant Coders (SQC), established in 2022 and founded by Dr. Vikramjot Singh.

The robot dog can respond to voice commands, detect obstacles, follow humans, and perform intelligent actions using Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, sensors, and embedded systems.
