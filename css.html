AI Powered Robot Dog – Chi-Chi

Chi-Chi is an AI-powered robotic dog developed by the technical club Super Quant Coders (SQC), established in 2022 and founded by Dr. Vikramjot Singh.

The robot dog demonstrates real-world applications of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, sensors, and embedded systems through intelligent movement and environment interaction.
